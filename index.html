Game Development

Transcontinental

Transcontinental is a narrative-based game about a 17-year-old girl traveling back and forth between Washington, DC and California on a train during summer. She finds insight and solace from conversations with strangers along the way, but also a few cold shoulders.

(Work in progress!)

Art

(h)ours

(h)ours is a fictionalized memoir of online roleplaying on forums in grade school. It is about the fantastical worlds can children construct collaboratively even when their conceptions of each other are purely virtual, and these worlds are juxtaposed with the banalities of childhood.

(Work in progress!)
